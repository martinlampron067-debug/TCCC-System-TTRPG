The **TCCC System** is a high-stakes, realistic tabletop role-playing engine designed for tactical depth and cinematic storytelling. Created by **Martin Lampron**, a Canadian Armed Forces Veteran, this system moves away from traditional "Rule-Lite" tropes toward a **Realistic Toolkit** that prioritizes simulation-grade precision without sacrificing fluid play.

---

## 🛠️ Core Mechanics (Intelligence Report)

* **Scalable Dice Resolution:** No flat d20 probability. Attributes and Skills use a **d4 to d12 scale**. As an operative improves, their potential for greatness scales exponentially rather than linearly.
* **Injury Over Hit Points:** Traditional "health bars" are eliminated. Damage is tracked via **Injury Boxes** (Stun, Severe, Lethal), where wounds apply direct malus penalties to character effectiveness in real-time.
* **Tactical Leverage:** Combat focuses on positioning, suppression, and cover. It is a "Countdown" system rather than a turn-based system, creating a fluid "Combat Dance."
* **Genre-Agnostic Engine:** A universal toolkit designed for all eras—from primitive survival to high-tech space opera.

## 📚 Current Modules & Expansions

### [Saeculum: Blood of Ages](https://martinlampron.github.io/TCCC-System/saeculum-details.html)
The core engine. Designed for modular play where survival depends on tactical safety and gear management rather than hit point attrition.

### [Saeculum: Carneferreus](https://martinlampron.github.io/TCCC-System/carneferreus-details.html)
A cyberpunk expansion set in a fractured, post-apocalyptic North America. Features gritty **"Data Hustle"** cracking mechanics and the **"Nature Cost"** of cybernetic augmentation.

### [TCCC Lite: The Blackjack System](https://martinlampron.github.io/TCCC-System/blackjack.html)
A zero-prep, cinematic version of the engine. Perfect for one-shots or fast-paced Western scenarios like *Trouble at the Black Jack Saloon*. Uses a "Push your Luck" blackjack-style resolution.

## 🌐 Description du Projet (FR)

Le **Système TCCC** est un moteur de jeu de rôle sur table réaliste et exigeant. Conçu par Martin Lampron, vétéran des Forces Armées Canadiennes, ce système privilégie la profondeur tactique et la précision opérationnelle.

* **Résolution Évolutive:** Utilisation de dés de d4 à d12 selon le rang de compétence.
* **Gestion des Blessures:** Remplacement des "points de vie" par un suivi réaliste des traumatismes physiques.
* **Bilingue:** Tous les modules sont conçus pour être joués en Français et en Anglais.
