The Lite Philosophy

TCCC Lite is a streamlined adaptation of the parent engine found in Saeculum. It is designed to be a "fast-filler"—perfect for one-shots or sessions where you want tactical depth without the weight of a 300-page manual.

Blackjack Resolution

Unlike standard d20 systems, the Lite engine often utilizes a "push your luck" style resolution. This creates immediate tension: do you play it safe or risk a "bust" for a critical cinematic success?

Trouble at the Black Jack Saloon

The premier module for the TCCC Lite system, transporting players to the unforgiving grit of the Old West.

No "HP Bloat"

In this system, you don't have a massive pool of health. Every shot counts. Wounds are tracked specifically, and a single well-placed bullet can permanently alter your character's effectiveness for the rest of the encounter.

Veteran-Informed Mechanics

Drawing from the author's 18 years of service in the Canadian Armed Forces, the combat mechanics focus on Tactical Leverage. Positioning, cover, and the weight of your choices matter more than just the numbers on your sheet.

Key Features

  • Bilingual Support: Fully playable in both English and Français.
  • Zero-Prep: Designed to get you from character creation to the first shootout in minutes.
  • Cinematic Lethality: Encourages creative solutions to avoid the deadly consequences of direct conflict.
