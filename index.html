SAECULUM: BLOOD OF AGES (CORE ENGINE)

A genre-agnostic tactical engine where skill isn't just a bonus—it's a mathematical advantage. Designed for Consequence-Heavy gameplay.

SCALABLE DICE RESOLUTION: Standard tests use 1d20, but as skill increases, players roll additional dice (1d4, 1d6, 1d8, 1d10, 1d12). High skill creates a predictable Bell Curve, favoring competence over random luck.
THE "TIERED" DEFENSE: Armor doesn't just block; it mitigates. Combat involves Opposed Rolls where margins of success determine the lethality of the strike.
TACTICAL CONDITIONING: Health is more than a number. Damage results in Conditions (Wounded, Bleeding, Shock) that directly impact your effectiveness in real-time.
