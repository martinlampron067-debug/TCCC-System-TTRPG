SAECULUM: BLOOD OF AGES (CORE ENGINE)

A genre-agnostic toolkit designed for every type of adventure. From gritty historical realism to far-future tactical operations, this system provides the tools for high-stakes play without the bloat.

SCALABLE DICE RESOLUTION: No d20s. Attributes and Skills are represented by dice ranging from d4 up to d12. As your expertise grows, your dice scale, creating a predictable Bell Curve that rewards true competence over random chance.
INJURY OVER HIT POINTS: Traditional health bars are gone. Instead, track every impact through a realistic injury system ranging from Stun to Lethal. Damage directly impacts your effectiveness in real-time.
GENRE-AGNOSTIC ADVENTURE: The system is built as a universal toolkit. Whether you are running a desert survivalist campaign or an urban tactical raid, the mechanics adapt to the setting while keeping the math consistent.
